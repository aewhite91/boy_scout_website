The Life of Troy

Troy is widely considered one of the greatest defensive players in NFL history. Known for his athleticism, leadership, and iconic long hair, Polamalu became a fan favorite during his career with the Pittsburgh Steelers.

Throughout his football career, Troy earned respect for his incredible instincts, hard work, and dedication both on and off the field. This page highlights his early life, football career, and some of his most notable achievements.

Early Life

Troy Polamalu, an American football player for the Pittsburgh Steelers, was born April 19, 1981 in Garden Grove, California. He had many acheivments such as making it to the Super Bowl and being put into the Hall of Fame, he started a football career at an early age which contributed to his successplaying for the Steelers as a saftey before retiring.

Football Career

Troy Palamalu had a great high school and college football career, he was drafted to play for the Steelers.

  • Troy went to Douglas High School in Winston, oregon. he was an excellent athlete and great in gridiron football.
  • He commited to play football at the University of Southern California (USC) aftrer receiving a scholarship.
  • While at (USC) he was a linebacker and a saftey.
  • After the Steelers Drafted troy he intercepted a pass and returned the ball 40 yards for a touchdown, his score helped seal a close victory for the Steelers and send them back to theb Super Bowl.
  • He retired after the 2014 season with career totals of 783 tackles, 32 interceptions, 14 forced fumbles, and 12 sacks.

Notable Achievements

Troy Palamalu was one of the Steelers best saftey, was a record breaking player, and made it into the hall of fame. Troy now spends his time with his family and now resides in Pittsburgh.
