Troop Activities

Outdoor adventures and learning experiences.

Fishing

Fishing teaches scouts patience, responsibility, and outdoor survival skills. Scouts learn how to safely handle fishing equipment, identify local fish species, and respect wildlife and nature while enjoying time outdoors with fellow troop members.

Camping

Camping is one of the most important scouting activities. Scouts learn how to set up tents, cook outdoors, build campfires safely, and work together as a team. Camping trips also help scouts build confidence and independence while enjoying nature.

Canoeing

Canoeing helps scouts develop teamwork, coordination, and water safety skills. Scouts learn proper paddling techniques, how to safely navigate waterways, and the importance of communication while working together in a canoe.
